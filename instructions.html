How to Play:

You will be shown two cards. Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card.

  • Get 10 coins to win the game.
  • One wrong guess and you lose.
  • Ace is the lowest (1.)
  • Jester is bigger than 10 but smaller than Queen.
  • King is the highest.
