  • Game

You will be shown two cards. You have to guess whether the next card is going to be higher or lower than the card you're shown. The objective is to get 10 coins by getting the most rights in a row. If you get one wrong, you lose the game. For those who'll need it: A is '1.' 'Jester' is higher than ten, but smaller than Queen. The king card is the biggest number.
